Our story

Started in a home kitchen. Still runs like one.

Saya's Cakes n Bakes began with weekend orders for friends and neighbours, and grew because people kept asking for "just one more." Every cake here is still made to order — no walk-in freezer, no shortcuts — using real butter, real cream and whatever fruit is actually in season around Chennai.
